Meet Indoree Poha

At Indoree Poha, we believe that great food doesn't just satisfy hunger it brings people together. Inspired by the streets of Indore, we bring you the comfort and flavor of home in every bite.

  • We use fresh, locally sourced ingredients to prepare authentic Poha, and other light bites.
  • Our menu is crafted with care, offering a variety of healthy and tasty options perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a quick snack.
  • From classic Kanda Poha to our fusion favorites like Cheese Poha and Schezwan Poha, there's something for everyone.

Whether you're craving a nostalgic taste of Indore or simply looking for a wholesome, fulfilling meal, Indoree Poha is your go-to spot. We’re here to serve you food that’s fast, fresh, and full of heart.
